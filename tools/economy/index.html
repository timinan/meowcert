Faucet 1 — visitor plays. The primary earn. Reward calculator below.
Faucet 2 — host collect pot. 25% royalty on every visitor reward, cap 300/day. "Your show earned N while you were away — COLLECT."
Faucet 3 — daily quests + login streak. 3 rotating dailies (all 3 = Standard box of choice); 7-day streak (day 7 = Golden box).
Faucet 4 — weeklies, achievements, community, events. Golden boxes; gold-tier achievements are the only earned Mythics.
Valve 1 — per-chart decay. Same chart same day: 100% → 50% → 25% floor.
Valve 2 — daily play-income budget. First 1200 coins/day from plays pay full, beyond that 10%. The master valve.
Valve 3 — own shows pay 0. Owners can play their own show (leaderboard + playsOnOwnShow stat) but earn no coins and no self-royalty.
UI honesty. When a valve triggers, the summary shows a "reduced reward" chip. Never silently pay less.
